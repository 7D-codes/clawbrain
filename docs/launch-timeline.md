## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Technical issues at launch | Code freeze 1 week before, thorough testing |
| Low Product Hunt performance | Rally community, prepare backup plan |
| Competitor launches | Focus on differentiation, don't panic |
| Burnout | Schedule rest days, sustainable pace |
| Low initial traction | Have 3-month runway to iterate |
